Beauty Blog

A minimal editorial-style platform that merges UX and beauty storytelling. Designed with emphasis on clarity, rhythm, and sensory calm — a modern digital magazine.

A short walkthrough of the Beauty Blog layout, smooth scroll, and interactive transitions.

Description

Beauty Blog is a responsive web project that combines editorial aesthetics with modern UX. It focuses on clean navigation, typographic hierarchy, and immersive imagery to create an atmosphere that feels personal yet professional — like a digital magazine for skincare and self-expression.

Concept

The concept bridges visual design and content strategy. Beauty Blog reimagines how beauty content can live online: not as product advertising but as reflective, story-driven experiences. Posts flow through gently timed scroll animations and subtle tonal shifts.

Design Process

The design uses a neutral palette — ivory tones with soft blush accents — for elegance and warmth. Typography follows an editorial rhythm: bold geometric headings paired with clean sans body text. The layout adapts across devices via a flexible grid, ensuring accessibility without sacrificing refinement.
