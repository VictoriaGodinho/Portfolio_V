Journey Across Borders

An interactive p5.js game inspired by my personal experience moving between Brazil, Chile, and the United States. It explores how crossing borders shapes identity, culture, and belonging through playful narrative levels.

A short gameplay demo showcasing the game’s interactive storytelling and visual transitions.

Description

Journey Across Borders is a reflective interactive game that mirrors my personal story of migration and adaptation. Created with p5.js, it invites players to navigate different symbolic environments that represent my experiences growing up in Brazil, living in Chile, and settling in New York City. Each level combines playful mechanics and visual metaphors to explore themes of belonging and identity.

Design Process

The game unfolds across three levels, each inspired by a place I’ve lived. The first level, Brazil, features vibrant beach tones and a soccer ball avatar, a nod to national culture. The second, Chile, shifts into cool snowy hues referencing the Cordillera de los Andes. The final, USA, combines urban doodles of New York City landmarks with complementary purple and green tones to capture the city’s rhythm and multicultural pulse.

Level 1 - Brazil: Bright beaches and a floating soccer ball avatar.

Level 2 - Chile: Snowy mountains inspired by the Andes where I used to ski.

Level 3 - USA: A colorful, bustling environment inspired by New York City.
